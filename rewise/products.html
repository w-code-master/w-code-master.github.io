Siberian Larch (Larix Sibirica) is a coniferous tree which is widely spread throughout the north of Russian Asia. Angaro-Baikal Larch (Siberian Larch) is the most famous type of larch, as harsh cold winters (down to -40°C) and warm summers (up to +40°C) create ideal growing conditions for the tree.
